Kebap

What is Kebap?

Kebap is a very famous and loved Turkish food. It has many different recipes but Today, we are focusing on one of its most famous and well-known types. It is commonly found in Turkey and every city and people in each region of Turkey add their own touch to it. .

Ingredients for Kebap

  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Tail fat
  • Garlic
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato gravy
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chili powder

Steps for Cooking Kebap

  1. Chop up onion and bell peppers
  2. Add them into ground meat
  3. Add salt and black pepper and knead them and leave them to rest
  4. Thread this onto skewers
  5. Give this to kebap shape
  6. Cook them until they are ready
  7. Place the kebap on breads and serve with tomate, pepper and onion
