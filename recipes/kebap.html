Kebap

What is Kebap?

Kebap is a very famous and loved Turkish food. It has many different recipes but today we are focusing one of the famous and well-known type of it. It's just called kebap in my city. It is commonly found in Turkey and every city and people in Turkey add something from their own to it.

Ingredients For Kebap

  • Ground meat
  • Onion
  • Tail fat
  • Garlic
  • Bell pepper
  • Tomato gravy
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Chili powder
