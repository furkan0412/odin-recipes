Tantuni

Turkish Tantuni

Tantuni is a Turkish dish I, myself, taste very late but I loved it the moment I first taste it. Since then, I try to eat it whenever possible. It's commonly found in Turkey but city of Mersin is famous for its tantuni. Tantuni can be made with both white meat and red meat. Red meat is a heavier than white meat tantuni but both of them taste amazing.
